Collection Spotlight

Just Arrived

Spotlight Book

Go set a watchman : a novel
by Harper Lee
Come to the MCNY Library to read one of the most awaited, and most controversial, books of 2015. For many, the story and lessons of "To Kill A Mockingbird" are an indelible part of their childhood. "Go Set A Watchman" returns to the tiny town of Maycomb, Alabama, 20 years later, exploring how the adult Scout's relationship with her father, the legendary Atticus Finch, has been transformed by the cruicible years of the civil rights movement. Whatever you think of the way it was published, this is the summer novel that everyone is talking about.
